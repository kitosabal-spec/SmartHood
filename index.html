About Us

A Community Built on
Trust & Harmony

San Alfonso Homes is a residential subdivision located in Pacol, Naga City, Camarines Sur. Founded in 2005, we are committed to providing a safe, clean, and well-maintained living environment for all our residents.

Our homeowners' association actively manages community affairs — from collecting dues and maintaining common areas, to resolving concerns and organizing neighborhood events.

Transparent financial management
Regular community meetings
Dedicated security personnel
Prompt maintenance response

Residential Lots

Spacious lots across 4 blocks with wide roads and proper drainage systems.

Green Environment

Tree-lined streets and landscaped areas for a refreshing neighborhood feel.

Secure Access

Controlled entry points with guard-on-duty 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Active HOA

Elected officers who genuinely care for the welfare of every homeowner.
